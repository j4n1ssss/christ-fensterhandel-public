    - Tablet (md): StepContent full width, PreviewPanel below
    - Mobile (sm): MobileStepHeader on top, StepContent below, PreviewPanel below, StepNavigation as sticky footer (fixed bottom-0)
    Reads currentStep from store, renders the appropriate step component in StepContent area. For now, StepContent renders placeholder text "Step {n} — {name}" for steps without implementations yet.

    - Future: Greyed out, NOT clickable
    Hidden on mobile (< lg breakpoint).

    'use client'. Receives currentStep from store. Maps step number to step component. Steps 1-10 initially render placeholder divs with step name. Actual step components will be implemented in Plans 02-03, 02-04, 02-05.
